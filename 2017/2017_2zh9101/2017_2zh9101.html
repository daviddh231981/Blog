Shenzhen Airlines's First A330! Shenzhen to Beijing

Quick photo report of Shenzhen Airlines's first A330-300, which they got a few months ago and are flying domestically.

Wing view from 18K wasn't too bad.

Here's the newspaper article talking about their second A330 that they just received the day before my flight.

Paper cups... at least there was branding?

Entertainment screen was also quite new.

Shenzhen Airlines is allegedly a Low-Cost Carrier. Well it feels like they aren't because a full meal was served. It was good, though my expectations were low. Noodles were the entree.

Cabin with mood lighting.

Spoilers deployed to slow down before landing.

Here's a picture of the plane at Beijing. The registration was B-8865. I think the ZH livery on the A330 looks nice.

A nice flight with Shenzhen Airlines. I would not hesitate to take this flight again if it fit my schedule.
