China Southern A321 Beijing to Guangzhou

Quick photo report of a China Southern A321 domestically. It's the earliest flight out of there as far as I'm aware, seeing it departs right around 6:30. Not helpful if you're far from the airport.

B-6553 will fly CZ3166 today. It's in the Skyteam livery. So that's a bonus to the flight today.

Channels with audio. I guess I wanted to sleep for the flight today, so it didn't matter?

Legroom was acceptable, it was only 3 hours, and it's an A321. Though I planned to sleep.

Otherwise it's a dropdown TV with their programming of whatever it is. I just preferred sleeping and reading my book.

A meal was served. Both options were rice, but one's chicken and the other's beef. It came with bread, ham and veggie combo, and some other things I couldn't tell.

I had the chicken and rice, which was nice.

Here is the beef option.

Since it's lunch that was being served, I had a coke with ice.

It's daytime though, and I could actually hear the IAE V2500 engines hum.

It's early, so I would likely find a place near the airport to spend the night before the flight. The terminal is empty and calmer around this time too. I would not hesitate to take this flight again if it fit my schedule.
