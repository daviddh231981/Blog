United Express E175 in First Class


After getting some sleep and changing into different pants (because it's freezing), I went across the concourse to my gate, from B17 to B1. Thank goodness for boarding notifications!


I had a first class seat in the first class cabin. Unfortunately, since boarding was rushed and I was extremely exhausted, I only got this photo.


I'm so thankful that a blanket was offered, especially since I wanted to sleep. I fell asleep roughly a few minutes after takeoff.


Legroom is decent enough for me.


And of course, an amazing sunrise on takeoff.

No Predeparture Beverage was offered today, but whatever. I was a bit annoyed, though, since I would've liked to have a coffee before it kicked in for the day. But one shouldn't really rely on inflight food and beverage. Still doesn't make it any better, though.


We landed early, and I made a beeline to the parking lot. Screenshot from the United App.

By the time that scheduled arrival time rolled around, I was off airport property and well on the drive home.
